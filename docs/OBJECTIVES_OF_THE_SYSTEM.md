# Objectives of the System

## General Objective

The general objective of the study is to design and develop a web-based appointment booking, quotation, work job, payment, and monitoring system with augmented reality support for SOG Glass and Aluminum. The system aims to improve the management of customer appointments, product quotation requests, work job scheduling, worker assignments, payment tracking, warranty records, and customer communication through a centralized digital platform.

## Specific Objectives

Specifically, the system aims to:

1. Develop a customer appointment booking module that allows clients to request inspections or services by providing their contact information, preferred schedule, service address, and optional quotation items.

2. Provide a digital quotation module where customers can select products, view quotation summaries, review item images, approve quotation items, and sign approved quotations electronically.

3. Create an admin appointment management module that allows authorized personnel to create, view, update, confirm, reschedule, cancel, reopen, and monitor appointments.

4. Develop a work job management module that enables admins to create work jobs from confirmed appointments or direct service requests, assign workers, update job progress, and record job-related remarks.

5. Implement a worker module that allows assigned staff to view their schedules, monitor assigned appointments and work jobs, update allowed job statuses, and upload before-and-after job photos.

6. Provide a payment management module that supports downpayment and full payment tracking, PayPal sandbox payment processing, manual cash payment recording, additional charges, refunds, and payment reconciliation.

7. Create a warranty record module that automatically issues warranty records for completed work jobs and helps track warranty coverage, duration, status, and terms.

8. Develop a customer dashboard where clients can view their appointments, work jobs, quotations, payment status, warranty information, activity logs, and service progress in one place.

9. Implement real-time notifications and database notifications to inform customers, admins, and workers about important updates such as appointment changes, quotation signing, work job progress, payment updates, and customer actions.

10. Provide an audit log module that records important system activities such as login, logout, OTP requests, record creation, updates, deletions, payment actions, and status changes for security and accountability.

11. Develop reporting and dashboard features that allow admins to view business-related information such as appointments, work jobs, payments, sales, additional charges, customer satisfaction, and operational performance.

12. Integrate an augmented reality measurement and product preview module that allows customers to visualize glass and aluminum products in a real-world environment and use captured measurements as part of the quotation request process.

13. Ensure that the system applies role-based access control for customers, admins, sub-admins, and workers so that each user can only access the features and records appropriate to their role.

14. Improve data accuracy and user experience by applying proper form validation, error handling, confirmation dialogs, responsive design, and user-friendly interfaces across customer, admin, and worker modules.

15. Generate printable and exportable records such as quotation PDFs, sales reports, payment reports, and other documents needed for business operations and capstone documentation.
